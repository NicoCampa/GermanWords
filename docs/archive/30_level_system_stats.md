# 30-Level Progression System - 1 Year Journey

## Overview
The new 30-level system is designed for **365 days of daily usage** to reach the maximum level. The system is balanced to keep users engaged with early wins while providing long-term progression goals.

---

## 🎨 Skin Unlock Levels
Users unlock new Worty skins at these milestone levels:
- **Level 3** - First skin unlock (early reward)
- **Level 5** - Second skin
- **Level 10** - Third skin (end of beginner phase)
- **Level 15** - Fourth skin (mid-game milestone)
- **Level 20** - Fifth skin (veteran status)
- **Level 25** - Sixth skin (elite status)
- **Level 30** - Final ultimate skin (mastery achieved)

**Total Skins: 7** (including default starter skin)

## 🎯 Daily Activity Requirements (to reach Level 30 in 1 year)

### Target: ~30 XP per day average (11,000 XP ÷ 365 days = ~30.1 XP/day)

### Recommended Daily Routine:

#### **Option 1: Standard Daily Flow**
1. **View 3 new words** = 15 XP (5 XP each)
2. **Complete daily review quiz** (3 questions, aim for 2-3 correct) = 20-30 XP
3. **Bonus: Fully learn 1 word** (view 3 times) = +10-30 XP extra

**Daily Total: 35-60 XP** ✅ Above target

#### **Option 2: Minimal Engagement**
1. **View 2 new words** = 10 XP
2. **Complete daily review** (3 questions, 2 correct) = 20 XP

**Daily Total: 30 XP** ✅ Meets target

#### **Option 3: Weekend Catch-up**
If user misses weekdays:
- Weekdays (5 days): 20 XP/day = 100 XP
- Weekend (2 days): 50 XP/day = 100 XP
- **Weekly Total: 200-210 XP** ✅ On track

## 💡 Key Design Decisions

1. **Early Wins:** First skin unlock at Level 3 (just 4 days) to hook users
2. **Balanced Scaling:** XP requirements increase gradually, not exponentially
3. **Regular Rewards:** Skin unlocks spaced every ~50-80 days for sustained motivation
4. **Achievable Goal:** 30 XP/day is very reasonable (3 words + 1 quiz)
5. **Flexibility:** Users can catch up on weekends if they miss weekdays
6. **No Pressure:** Even at 25 XP/day, users reach Level 22 in a year
7. **Final Push:** Level 30 requires dedication but is absolutely achievable
